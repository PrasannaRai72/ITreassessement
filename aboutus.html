About Us

We provide single, double and twin air-conditioned rooms with attached bathroom, free high speed internet (Wi-Fi). We will be happy to organize free airport pick-up service for you. Hotel Bubo Himalaya provides luggage storage, currency exchange and laundry services on request. Hotel Bubo Himalaya also provides all service related to travel.
